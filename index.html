Our Services

Fast Shipping Starts with Simplicity

LTL (Less Than Truck Load)

Our LTL service is designed for smaller shipments that don’t require a full truck. By sharing space with other shipments, you save on transportation costs while still enjoying reliable, on-time delivery. Ideal for businesses with frequent, medium-sized cargo needs.

PTL (Partial Truck Load)

PTL offers the perfect balance between speed and cost. You reserve a portion of the truck based on your shipment size, ensuring faster transit times and reduced handling compared to LTL, while still maintaining flexibility and affordability.

FTL (Full Truck Load) Shipping

With our FTL service, you get an entire truck dedicated exclusively to your shipment. This option guarantees maximum safety, minimal transit time, and complete control — perfect for bulk deliveries or time-sensitive goods.

White Glove Service

Our premium White Glove Service goes beyond standard delivery. From careful packaging and secure transportation to on-site unloading and setup, we ensure every step is handled with the highest level of care. Ideal for sensitive, high-value, or specialized shipments that demand extra attention.

FAQ

Everything You Should Know About Freight

  • Shipping & Delivery
  • Tracking & Support
  • Billing & Documentation
  • What services do you offer?
    We provide a full range of freight services including LTL, PTL, FTL, and White Glove delivery to meet different shipping needs.
    • LTL (Less-Than-Truckload): Ideal for smaller freight shipments that don’t require a full truck. Your cargo shares space with other loads, helping reduce shipping costs.
    • PTL (Partial Truckload): Suitable for medium-sized shipments that need more space than LTL but not an entire trailer, offering faster transit times and lower freight rates.
    • FTL (Full Truckload): Best for large shipments that fill a truck or require dedicated space. This service provides direct pickup-to-delivery with minimal handling.
    • White Glove Service: Premium delivery for fragile, high-value, or sensitive items, including inside delivery, careful handling, and full placement at the final destination.
    How do I get started?
    Getting started is easy. Just enter the pickup and drop-off ZIP codes, along with your package details (weight, dimensions, and freight type). Our system instantly provides a freight quote with no login or account required.
    Do you handle international shipments?
    Currently, we specialize in freight shipping across all U.S. states. We also occasionally handle shipments to Canada, providing reliable cross-border delivery when available.
    What is included in the shipping process?
    Our shipping process is simple and reliable. You choose the type of service you need, and we handle everything from pickup, careful cargo handling, to final delivery at your destination.
    How long does shipping usually take?
    Shipping time depends on the pickup and delivery locations. You’ll see an estimated transit time when you request your freight quote.
  • How can I track my shipment?
    We provide a tracking number for every shipment, allowing you to monitor the status and transit progress at any time.
    Do you provide real-time updates?
    Yes, we offer real-time shipment updates to keep you informed throughout the delivery process.
    What if my package is delayed?
    Delivery timelines typically range from 4–7 days, depending on the distance. If a delay occurs, our team will keep you informed with regular updates until the shipment is delivered.
    Can I change the delivery address after booking?
    To modify the delivery address, the shipment must be canceled and refunded. Once payment is completed, shipment details—including the address—cannot be changed. Please contact our team via live chat to initiate the cancellation and refund process.
    Do you offer customer support 24/7?
    Our customer support team is available Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM Pacific Time.
  • What payment methods do you accept?
    We accept Credit Card, ACH payments, and Zelle for your convenience.
    Do you provide shipping invoices?
    Yes. Once a shipment is booked and paid through the Lama Freight website, an invoice is automatically issued. If the shipment is arranged by our Lama Freight Team, we provide a Bill of Lading (BOL) prior to shipping, an invoice on the day of delivery, and payment receipts once the transaction is completed.
    How do customs duties and taxes work?
    At this time, customs duties and taxes do not apply to our services, as they are only required for international shipments.
    Can I request a cost breakdown?
    Yes. A detailed cost breakdown is provided during the payment process on the Lama Freight website.
    Do you offer credit terms for businesses?
    Yes. We offer monthly credit limits for eligible business clients.
